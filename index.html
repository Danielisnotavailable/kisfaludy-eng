Kisfaludy Guesthouse - Sümeg

About

The Kisfaludy Guesthouse, named after a great Hungarian poet Kisfaludy Sándor. Our guesthouse is located right in the heart of Sümeg.

If you feel like having a special taste of the Hungarian traditional cuisine, special wild dishes, nice wines from the region and of course, the famous Hungarian Pálinka, it is the right place to go!

You can visit the medieval Castle of Sümeg as well, but if you rather feel like taking a short trip to the lake Balaton just nearby, making an excursion in the beautiful natural environment, it is all possible.

Check out our Galery!

We have a charming garden, and a confectionery where you can enjoy your meal or just drink some refreshments in the hot summer nights.

We have 10 rooms available for 1, 2 or 4 persons.
